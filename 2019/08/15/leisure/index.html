Leisure

What is this life if, full of care,
We have no time to stand and stare?
No time to stand beneath the boughs,
And stare as long as sheep and cows.
No time to see, when woods we pass,
Where squirrels hide their nuts in grass.
No time to see, in broad daylight,
Streams full of stars, like skies at night.
No time to turn at Beauty’s glance,
And watch her feet, how they can dance.
No time to wait till her mouth can，
Enrich that smile her eyes began.
A poor life this if, full of care,
We have no time to stand and stare.

By William Henry Davies

If you ask me what I want when I was very young,you would get the answer “game machine, cable televison with mamy channels, computer with internet, movie Avatar“It is so boring that we hoped time goes fast and more fast at that period.

What did I do at that time?Imaged.Imaged that I was a anti-Japenese hero,thought why I came to this magical world,YY beautiful girls in my class.

However,I will never catch the feel “have nothing to do” any more.This long-standing desease of humanbeings has been cured by the popularity of smartphones and Internet.Once I feel idle less than a second, my smartphone, my life habit, my environment will remind me there are many intersting things in this big world that I can receive and I must receive.One day is not very long after the heavy work and study mission. If we are exploited by meaningless entertainment even more,we will find that we have not lived ourselves until death.

It doesn’t mean I hope to go back to the age of material scarcity,go back to an inaction slit ,but to grasp the life of myself without others’ lust and obsolete tradition.

Leisure is not an empty day with nothing to do.Leisure does not mean lazy and idle.Leisure is a condition ,that we can arrange our time to chase the dream and enjoy the life we can grasp,without any alienated mission the world imposes on us.
